What is Web Breakz?

Web Breakz is an app that increases your productivity by giving you breaks and quite literally yelling at you to get off the internet and get back to work.


Who can use Web Breakz?

Anyone and everyone! If you are a person cruising through the interwebs, feel free to use our app to get back to work, focus on what should be getting done, and have an app yell at you for cruising the interwebs for too long.


How long do I have until my next web break?

You decide! --or-- We yell at you every thirty minutes so you could be the most productive human you could ever be!
